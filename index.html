Stephen Hawking

Professor Stephen Hawking 1942 - 2018

Here's a timeline of genius Stephen Hawking's life:

  • Born in Oxford, England.
  • Begins university studies at Oxford.
  • Diagnosed at the age of 21 with the degenerative nerve disorder ALS, or Lou Gehrig's disease.
  • Marries his wife, Jane Wilde, with she had three children.
  • Publishes A Brief History of Time a book that raises that raises the theories of the big bang and radiation of black holes.
  • Experiments weightlessness on a two-hour trip in a Boering 727-200.
  • Presents the Breakthrough Starshot project with billionaire Yuri Milner, a project that proposes to launch nano-ships capable of traveling on exploration missions to Alpha Centauri.
  • Dies in Cambridge, United Kingdom at age 76.
